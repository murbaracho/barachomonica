Project Overview

This project explores customer demographics, spending patterns, and advertising effectiveness using Excel, SQL, Tableau, and R. The goal was to uncover insights that help the 2Market supermarket improve marketing strategy, product targeting, and customer segmentation.

3Ô∏è‚É£ Country-Level Spend & Ad Summary Dashboard

This dashboard shows country-level customer spending, response to social media ads, and the percentage of revenue from customers who converted via social media.

  • Spain leads with the highest total spend ($659,557) and social revenue share (36%).
  • Canada and South Africa follow, each with ~31% social revenue share.
  • Montenegro shows no conversions from social media ads.

These insights highlight where social ads are most effective and where to focus future campaigns.
